Our Courses

Open Water Diver

The Open Water Diver course is your first step into the exciting world of scuba diving. Learn the fundamentals of diving, including safety procedures, equipment use, and underwater skills.

Duration: 3-4 days
Prerequisites: None
Price: RM800.00

Advanced Open Water Diver

Take your diving skills to the next level with the Advanced Open Water Diver course. Explore new dive sites, enhance your underwater navigation skills, and experience night diving.

Duration: 2-3 days
Prerequisites: Open Water Diver certification
Price: RM950.00

Rescue Diver

The Rescue Diver course teaches you to prevent and manage diving emergencies. Gain the confidence to assist other divers in distress and become a more responsible diver.

Duration: 3-4 days
Prerequisites: Advanced Open Water Diver certification
Price: RM1,200.00
